# Quick Start: Notifications

## How to Enable Task Notifications

### Step 1: Open Settings
1. Look at the top-right corner of the app
2. Click the **three-dot menu (â‹®)**
3. Select **"Settings"** from the dropdown

### Step 2: Configure Notifications
In the Settings dialog, you'll see:

**Enable Notifications Toggle**
- Turn this ON to receive notifications
- Turn this OFF to disable all notifications

**Choose Your Reminder Time**
- Click one of the quick-select buttons:
  - **1 min** - Get notified 1 minute before
  - **5 mins** - Get notified 5 minutes before (recommended)
  - **10 mins** - Get notified 10 minutes before
  - **15 mins** - Get notified 15 minutes before
  - **30 mins** - Get notified 30 minutes before
  - **60 mins** - Get notified 1 hour before

**OR use the slider** for any custom time between 1-60 minutes

### Step 3: Save Your Settings
1. Click the **"Save"** button
2. You'll see a confirmation message at the bottom
3. All your tasks will now have notifications scheduled!
